<h2 id="current-lesson">HTML lesson 3 - Headings 2</h2>
			<h4 class="indent">We have used the &lt;h1> tag in our previous lessons but it's time to introduce the other heading tags. Headings are ordered by numbers with 1 being the largest and most important. headings degrade in size and importance with the number asigned to the them. example &lt;h1>&lt;h2>&lt;h3>&lt;h4>&lt;h5>&lt;h6>. CLick below for an example of all these heading tags.</h4>
			<h4 class="code">&lt;!DOCTYPE html>
&lt;html>
&lt;head>
&lt;/head>
&lt;body>
  &lt;h1>H1 heading&lt;/h1>
  &lt;h2>H2 heading&lt;/h2>
  &lt;h3>H3 heading&lt;/h3>
  &lt;h4>H4 heading&lt;/h4>
  &lt;h5>H5 heading&lt;/h5>
  &lt;h6>H6 heading&lt;/h6>
&lt;/body>
&lt;/html></h4>
