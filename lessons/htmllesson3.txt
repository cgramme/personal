<h2 id="current-lesson">HTML lesson 3 - Heading tags</h2>
			<h4 class="indent">Tags are the building blocks of HTML, so let's check out some common ones. We will start with the "heading" tag. Headings are used for text content and can define different text sizes and boldness. The largest heading tag looks like &lt;h1&gt;content&lt;/h1&gt;. Heading tags are numbered from 1 being the largest and scale down in size from 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 ect... The larger usually being used for page title and smaller headings for page content.</h4>
			<h4>We replaced the "Hello World" text from our last lesson with an h1 tag for the title.</h4>
			<h4 class="code">&lt;!DOCTYPE html>
&lt;html>
&lt;head>
  &lt;title>My Awesome Page Title&lt;/title>
&lt;/head>
&lt;body>
  &lth1>My first Website!&lt;/h1>	<----This is the h1 tag
&lt;/body>
&lt;/html></h4>
