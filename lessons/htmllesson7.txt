<h2 id="current-lesson">HTML lesson 7 - Inline style</h2>
			<h4 class="indent">All this text is cool but we want more! To make awesome changes to our text and everything else we will add "Style" to our code. Below in the &lt;h1> tag we have added a bit of style called "inline style". copy it to your notepad or just click on it to see it in action.</h4>
			<h4 class="code">&lt;!DOCTYPE html>
&lt;html>
&lt;head>
&lt;/head>
&lt;body>
  &lt;h1 style="color:red">Look at all this style&lt;/h1>
&lt;/body>
&lt;/html></h4>
