<h2 id="current-lesson">HTML lesson 9 - Intro to nesting</h2>
			<h4 class="indent">We have a nice box now, but let's see if we can put something in it. I've added a new tag inside our div, called the p tag or "paragraph" &lt;p>&lt;/p>. The p tag is used for long text like paragraphs or other lengthy text situations. All you have to do is put the &lt;p>&lt;/p> tags between our &lt;div>....&lt;/div> tags and we are set. Click to see the result!</h4>
			<h4 class="code">&lt;!DOCTYPE html>
&lt;html>
&lt;head>
&lt;/head>
&lt;body>
  &lt;div style="width:400px;height:200px;background-color:pink">
    &lt;p>I'm in a box!&lt;/p>            <---- "paragraph tag inside the div"
  &lt;/div>
&lt;/body>
&lt;/html></h4>
<div id="code-example" class="sample-code">
	<div style="width:400px;height:200px;background-color:pink"><p>I'm in a box!</p></div>
